TiksCam - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Summary

TiksCam does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works entirely on your local network.

Data Collection

None. TiksCam does not collect any personal data, usage information, analytics, or diagnostic data.

Camera and Microphone Access

TiksCam accesses your device's camera and microphone solely to stream video and audio over your local network to your computer. No images or audio are recorded, stored, or sent to external servers.

Local Network

The app uses your local network (WiFi or USB) to stream camera video to your computer. All communication happens directly between your iPhone and your computer, with no intermediaries.

Third Parties

TiksCam does not use any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose involving data collection.

Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
